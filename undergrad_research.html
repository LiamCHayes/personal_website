Technical Report

This tehcnical report looks at using change point analysis to understand the 2008 financial crisis. Completed over the summer of 2023.

Change point detection does seem like an accurate lagging indicator for stock movements. Using something like the CUSUM statistic could be helpful as a feature in a machine learning model trying to predict stock movements. It can also be used to better understand past events in the stock market.
