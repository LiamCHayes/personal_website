Classifying PT Exercises

In this in-progress project, we are using signal alignment techniques to identify when and what physical therapy exercise a person is doing as well as quality of the rep.

We have data from 5 sensors, each with an accelerometer, gyroscope, and magnemometer. Each of these gives data for x, y, and z axes. This means we have 45 different (although correlated) signals to process in order to identify each of the 8 exercises we have data for.
